Shantha Technical Institute – Wellington, The Nilgiris

Serving the community since 1974

Established in 1974 by Mrs. Santhakumari (W/O Mr. K.A. Vijayan), Shantha Technical Institute has been a trusted centre for Typewriting and Shorthand education in Wellington Bazaar, The Nilgiris, Tamil Nadu, for over five decades.

The institute continues its proud tradition of excellence, guiding generations of students in professional skill development and preparing them for Government of Tamil Nadu examinations.

Courses Offered

We provide specialized coaching in:

  • Typewriting (English & Tamil)
  • Shorthand (English & Tamil)

Typewriting students are trained through a structured method:

  • Fingering Practice (basic key familiarity)
  • Speed Practice (1st Paper)
  • Junior Grade – 30 words per minute
  • Senior Grade – 45 words per minute

The keyboard layout used for typewriting is identical to computer keyboards, enabling fast and accurate computer typing skills.

Duration & Timings

  • 1 hour per day:
    • Junior Grade: approx. 9 months
    • Senior Grade: additional 4 months
  • 2 hours per day (Fast Track) – Shorter completion time

Working Days

The only Typewriting Institute in the entire Nilgiris District that works six days a week since its inception.

Most other institutes operate only five days a week, observing Saturday and Sunday as holidays.

Shorthand Classes

Shorthand coaching is provided in both English and Tamil.

Tamil Shorthand is taught after completion of the theory portion in English for better clarity and transition.

Fees Structure

  • Monthly Fee: Rs. 750 (Government approved)
  • Admission Fee: Rs. 250 (one-time payment, even for rejoining students)
  • Same fees for all subjects and grades
  • Exam fees collected separately (slight variation, very reasonable compared to other Institutes)

  • Excellent hygiene & sanitation

  • Frequent Government and private mini-bus services
  • Nearby parking facilities for two-wheelers and four-wheelers

Next Step: Computer Office Automation (COA)

Students who pass the Typewriting Examination become eligible for the COA Exam conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu — an excellent qualification for both private and government sector employment.
